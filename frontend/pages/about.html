About Us

Building a Startup Ecosystem, One Founder at a Time

We are a local startup community created to empower founders through consistent meetups, brainstorming sessions, networking circles and real connections.

Our Story

Every great startup begins with a community, and every great community begins with people. We started with a simple idea: to have a thriving startup ecosystem in our hometown.

Over meetups in parking lots, informal gatherings, and brainstorming sessions — the movement grew. Today, we're a vibrant and active ecosystem that connects founders with the right people, ideas and opportunities.

Whether you're building your first startup or scaling your next venture, you'll find support, resources, and people who are more than happy to help.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower founders in our community by providing a dynamic forum for networking, knowledge sharing, mentoring and collaboration. We aim to educate our members about startup strategies, funding schemes, and other relevant knowledge, and to provide opportunities that inspire innovation and drive success.

Our Vision

To be a leading startup ecosystem where every founder, from grassroots to city level, can connect, learn, and grow, thereby fostering innovation and economic growth in the region.
