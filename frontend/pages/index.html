About Us

Building a Startup Ecosystem, One Founder at a Time

We are a local startup community created to empower founders through consistent meetups, brainstorming sessions, networking circles and real connections.

Every great startup begins with a community, and every great community begins with people. We started with a simple idea: to have a thriving startup ecosystem in our hometown.

Our Mission

To empower founders in our community by providing a dynamic forum for networking, knowledge sharing, mentoring and collaboration.

Our Vision

To be a leading startup ecosystem where every founder, from grassroots to city level, can connect, learn, and grow, fostering innovation and economic growth.
